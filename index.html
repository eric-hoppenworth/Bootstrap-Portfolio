About Me

“The important thing is not to stop questioning. Curiosity has its own reason for existence. One cannot help but be in awe when he contemplates the mysteries of eternity, of life, of the marvelous structure of reality. It is enough if one tries merely to comprehend a little of this mystery each day.”

-Albert Einstein

I know that quoting Einstein is a little cliché, but this particular quote rings true with me. I have always been of the curious sort, desperate to learn as much as I can about anything and everything.

That desperation has materialized in my many hobbies and interests. I am a chef; my specialty is not only in pan-Asian cuisine, but also Tex-Mex. I am a gardener; my small raised box plot in my front yard is literally bursting with produce each year. I am an educator; I tutor young people in maths and sciences, hoping to pass that curious spark onto them. I am a novice carpenter; I've built a few of the pieces in my home, hoping to eventual throw out all of my mass produced furniture.

I am a programmer. My cross-discipline knowledge gives me a leg up on design and problem solving. I face every challenge with the mindset that it will add another tool to my kit. I began with simple games to play with my friends on my home computer, and now I am looking outward. I am learning to take my creations to on-line platforms so that the world can see the advantages of an ever curious mind.
